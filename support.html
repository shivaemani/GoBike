Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock a booked bike?
Once you've reached the bike's location, open the GoBike app and click on "Active Rides". Tap the "Unlock" button to scan the QR code on the bike. The bike will unlock automatically.
What happens if I return the bike late?
Late returns are subject to additional charges based on your membership tier. We charge a standard penalty of ₹50 per hour of delay. Please try to modify your booking in advance if you need more time.
Can I get a refund if I cancel my booking?
Yes! Cancellations made 24 hours prior to the pickup time receive a 100% refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours may incur a small deduction. Refunds are credited to your wallet instantly.
What should I do in case of an accident?
Your safety is our priority. Please ensure you are safe and call emergency services if needed. Then, tap the "Emergency SOS" button in your active ride screen or call our 24/7 hotline immediately.
